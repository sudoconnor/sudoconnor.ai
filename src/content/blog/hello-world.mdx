# Hello, World

Welcome to **SudoConnor** — my corner of the internet where I write about the things I build, break, and learn.

## What is this?

This site is a place for me to document my work in **DevOps**, **platform engineering**, **security**, and **AI**. I've spent too many years solving problems and keeping the solutions locked in my head or buried in Slack threads. That ends here.

## What to expect

- **Infrastructure deep dives** — Kubernetes, Terraform, CI/CD pipelines, and cloud architecture.
- **Security engineering** — DevSecOps practices, pipeline hardening, and compliance automation.
- **AI experiments** — Agentic workflows, LLM integration, and how AI is changing the way we build software.
- **Honest write-ups** — Not polished tutorials, but real notes from real problems. What worked, what didn't, and why.

## Why "SudoConnor"?

Because sometimes you need elevated privileges to get things done. And because every good engineer knows: with great `sudo`, comes great responsibility.

```bash
$ sudo make things-better
[sudo] password for connor: ********
Building better infrastructure...
Done.
```
